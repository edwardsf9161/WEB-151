Rushing Balls

Gameplay

 Landing

Monitization

Rushing Balls has two playing modes: Endless and Normal.

You can share from the app directly to Facebook.

It is possible to acquire additional gems and remove advertising with in-app purchases.

The game becomes difficult to play as you keep rolling because the gems are placed in front of the black obstacles, and it is very hard to by-pass them.

You move along in the game collecting gems while avoiding black obstacles.

The game starts with 500 players. You pass other players as you go.

As you collect gems, the game increases speed, thus increasing difficulty of play.



Personally, I cannot last more than two minutes in level one playing this game.
