Gameplay

The developer of Rushing Balls, Game Battleground, has implemented the following inside the game to derive revenue:



The game offers in-app purchases of gems and to remove advertising.

In-app purchases range from $1.99 to $3.99 per item.

The game contains ads. The ads appear when you lose, and there are also ads which appear at the bottom of the screen during the game.

The game is rated E for everyone with over 500,000 installs and has over 2000 reviews with a 4.1 star ratings.
